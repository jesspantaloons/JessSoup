Jess Soup

A recipe for making Jess happy and warm exactly like the feeling of eating soup.

Ingredients

  • A handmade quilt
  • Large canvas "cute" tote bag
  • A blackberry "Clean" Yerba Mate
  • A warm spot in the sun
  • A long walk
  • Laughter
  • Arlo and Dan
  • Assortment of interesting snacks

Instructions

  1. Check weather, is the sun out? if so proceed to step two.
  2. Pack quilt and assortment of snacks in tote bag, whatever you do, don't forget the Yerba Mate.
  3. Gather Arlo and Dan and walk out the front door.
  4. Keep walking, this is meant to be a "long walk" good to identify plants along the way.
  5. Keep walking until you find a nice warm spot to lay your quilt down on.
  6. Bring out those snacks and have a little picnic! Jokes and laughter are greatly encouraged.
  7. Eat and laugh until soul is full and warm just like a fresh bowl of soup.
